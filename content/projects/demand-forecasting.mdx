## Overview

Built an end-to-end demand forecasting system for a retail client that processes historical sales data, seasonality patterns, and real-time inventory levels to generate accurate demand predictions.

## The Challenge

Traditional forecasting models ignored inventory constraints, leading to phantom demand signals when products were out of stock. This caused a feedback loop where understocked items appeared to have low demand.

## Solution Architecture

The system consists of three major components:

1. **Data Pipeline** — ETL process that ingests sales transactions, inventory snapshots, and external signals (weather, holidays, promotions)
2. **ML Engine** — Ensemble of LSTM and XGBoost models with a custom inventory-censoring layer
3. **Dashboard** — React-based UI for business users to visualize forecasts, adjust parameters, and export reports

## Key Results

- **35% reduction** in mean absolute percentage error (MAPE) compared to the previous rule-based system
- **$2.1M annual savings** in reduced overstock and stockout costs
- Processing **50,000+ SKUs** with daily forecast refreshes in under 15 minutes

## Technical Highlights

- Implemented a custom loss function that accounts for inventory censoring
- Built an A/B testing framework to compare model versions in production
- Containerized with Docker and deployed on AWS ECS with auto-scaling
